Yes. ChatNest reads your existing conversation list and lets you organize them immediately — no changes to your chats.

Completely. All folders and tags are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. Nothing leaves your device.

Any Chromium-based browser: Chrome, Brave, Edge, Opera, and more.
